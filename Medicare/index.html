About Us

In our commitment to bridging the gap to better healthcare for all, we go beyond information dissemination. Our platform acts as a holistic resource, empowering individuals and communities to navigate the complexities of healthcare accessibility. Beyond detailing government healthcare schemes, we foster practical connections by linking users with non-governmental organizations (NGOs) that extend hands-on support. These NGOs play a crucial role in providing practical assistance, ensuring that individuals not only understand the available healthcare initiatives but also receive guidance throughout the application process.
Our vision is rooted in the belief that everyone, regardless of their financial background, should have the opportunity to lead a healthy and prosperous life. By seamlessly connecting users with relevant NGOs, we strive to make healthcare more than just a concept but a tangible reality. We understand that navigating the healthcare system can be daunting, and our mission is to make this journey more manageable, ensuring that individuals not only have access to information but also receive the support they need to take full advantage of available healthcare opportunities.

Ayushman Bharat

Ayushman Bharat is the Government of India’s flagship health insurance scheme launched in 2017 to provide Universal Health Coverage (UHC).The scheme has two components HWC and PM-JAY.

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)

This scheme offered by the Ministry of Labour and Employment provides healthcare protection and coverage to Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.
